üöÄ Getting Started

How do I set up my first garden?

Step-by-Step Guide:

1. Open PlantBuddy AI and tap "My Gardens"
2. Tap the "+" button to create a new garden
3. Enter your garden name (e.g., "Indoor Plants", "Backyard Garden")
4. Choose your garden type (Indoor, Outdoor, Greenhouse, etc.)
5. Add your first plant by tapping "Add Plant"

How do I add plants to my garden?

Two Ways to Add Plants:

Method 1 - Plant ID: Use AI to identify unknown plants
Method 2 - Manual Entry: Search our plant database

For unknown plants, we recommend using Plant ID for accurate identification and automatic care recommendations.

What information should I provide about my plants?

For the best care recommendations, provide:

  • Plant name (use Plant ID if unknown)
  • Planting date or age
  • Current location (indoor/outdoor, room, etc.)
  • Any visible issues or concerns

üîç Plant Identification

How do I identify a plant using Plant ID?

Using Plant ID Feature:

1. Go to "More" tab ‚Üí "AI Features" ‚Üí "Plant ID"
2. Tap "Take Photo" or "Choose from Library"
3. Take a clear photo of the plant (see photography tips below)
4. Wait for AI analysis (usually 10-30 seconds)
5. Review the identification results and care recommendations

What types of plants can Plant ID identify?

Plant ID can identify:

  • Houseplants and indoor plants
  • Garden flowers and ornamental plants
  • Vegetables and herbs
  • Trees and shrubs
  • Succulents and cacti
  • Common weeds and wildflowers
For best results, focus on distinctive features like leaves, flowers, or unique plant parts.

How accurate is Plant ID?

Plant ID uses advanced AI technology and typically achieves:

  • 90%+ accuracy for common houseplants
  • 85%+ accuracy for garden plants
  • 80%+ accuracy for wild plants and weeds

Accuracy depends on photo quality, plant distinctiveness, and lighting conditions.

üì∏ Photography Tips

How to take the best plant photos for identification?

Photo Tips for Plant ID:

Lighting: Use natural light when possible, avoid harsh shadows
Focus: Focus on distinctive features like leaves, flowers, or bark
Angle: Take photos from multiple angles if possible
Background: Use a plain background to avoid distractions
Stability: Hold your phone steady or use a tripod

What should I photograph for disease detection?

Disease Detection Photos:

1. Take close-up photos of affected areas
2. Include both healthy and unhealthy parts
3. Photograph leaves from both top and bottom
4. Include photos of stems and roots if possible
5. Ensure good lighting to show true colors

Common photography mistakes to avoid

  • Blurry or out-of-focus images
  • Too dark or too bright lighting
  • Including too much background clutter
  • Taking photos from too far away
  • Photographing only one small part of the plant

üìÖ Care Calendar Help

How do I set up plant care reminders?

Setting Up Care Reminders:

1. Go to "My Gardens" and select a plant
2. Tap "Care Calendar" or "Set Reminders"
3. Choose care tasks (watering, fertilizing, pruning, etc.)
4. Set frequency and timing
5. Enable notifications in Settings

What types of care reminders can I set?

  • Watering: Daily, weekly, or custom intervals
  • Fertilizing: Monthly or seasonal schedules
  • Pruning: Seasonal or growth-based timing
  • Repotting: Annual or size-based reminders
  • Pest Check: Weekly health inspections

How do I adjust care schedules for different seasons?

PlantBuddy AI automatically adjusts care recommendations based on:

  • Seasonal changes in your location
  • Indoor vs. outdoor growing conditions
  • Plant dormancy periods
  • Weather patterns and temperature changes
Enable location services for automatic seasonal adjustments to your care calendar.

üîß Troubleshooting

Plant ID is not working or giving poor results

Common Solutions:

1. Check your internet connection
2. Ensure you have remaining daily scans (Basic Mode)
3. Try taking a clearer photo with better lighting
4. Focus on distinctive plant features
5. Try different angles or parts of the plant

App is running slowly or crashing

  • Close and restart the app
  • Check for app updates in the App Store
  • Restart your device
  • Ensure you have sufficient storage space
  • Check your internet connection

Notifications are not working

Fix Notification Issues:

1. Go to iPhone Settings ‚Üí Notifications ‚Üí PlantBuddy AI
2. Ensure "Allow Notifications" is enabled
3. Check that "Sounds" and "Badges" are enabled
4. Verify "Time Sensitive" is enabled for care reminders

Data is not syncing between devices

  • Ensure you're signed in with the same Apple ID
  • Check your iCloud storage space
  • Verify iCloud is enabled for PlantBuddy AI
  • Try signing out and back in
